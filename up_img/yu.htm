EUCs Co-op Program New in Fall 2024

EUC degrees have a Co-op option. Students in EUCs undergraduate programs can declare their intention to participate in the co-op program from the beginning. Like other Co-op programs, EUCs program consists of at least two work terms in paid positions, for a total of 12-16 months of work terms total, interspersed with study terms. There are also two required courses one at the beginning and one at the end of the program. Successful students receive a Co-op annotation on their transcript and graduate with meaningful work experiences and contacts.

EUCs 3+2 Degree Pathways

The EUC 3+2 pathways link together 3 years of undergraduate study in Cities, Regions, Planning (BES), Environmental Arts & Justice (BES), Global Geography (BA) or Sustainable Environmental Management (BES) with two years of graduate study in the Master in Environmental Studies (MES) program. 

LEARN MORE

NEW! Pathways to the Master of Management (MMgt) Your EUC Degree + 1-year at the Schulich School of Business

In partnership with the Schulich School of Business, EUC is excited to announce the launch of the Pathway to Master of Management (MMgt) providing high achieving students the opportunity to link their fourth year EUC degree program to a one-year MMgt degree, growing their knowledge to open doors for new career opportunities post-graduation. Eligible for ALL degree programs offered by EUC.
